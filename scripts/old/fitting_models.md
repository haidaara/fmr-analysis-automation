#### 1) Symmetric Lorentzian (with linear baseline)
\[
F_{\text{sym}}(H)= 
A\,\frac{\Delta H^{2}}{(H-H_{0})^{2}+\Delta H^{2}} 
\;+\; C \;+\; D\,H
\]

---

#### 2) Asymmetric Lorentzian (exactly as in the lab model)
\[
F_{\text{asym}}(H)=
A\frac{\Delta H^{2}}{(H-H_{0})^{2}+\Delta H^{2}}
\;+\;
B\frac{\Delta H\,(H-H_{\mathrm{res}})}{(H-H_{0})+\Delta H^{2}}
\;+\; C \;+\; D\,H
\]

---

\[
\begin{aligned}

\]
